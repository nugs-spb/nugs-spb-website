Introduction

The most enviable city in the world is undoubtedly saint Petersburg- Russia ranging from her captivative historic architecture to its amorous stories.

The National union of Ghana students - Petersburg (Nugs-Spb) takes pride in providing a larger umbrella that gathers all Ghanaians in the city for companionship and great fun. The union offers golden opportunities to the general student body which includes : sponsorship, summer jobs, summer camps, football competitions, educational seminars, annual get together.

NUGS SPB is a must join student union

Daniel K. Oppong
President
Nugs Spb
