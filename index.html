Introduction

Knowing how quick the number of Ghanaians was incredibly increasing in Saint Petersburg for the purposes of education and work, there was the need to bring all of us( Ghanaians) under one umbrella; the Ghana Community Saint Petersburg. Although around same time the student body was much organized and had formed NUGS Petersburg( in the year 2008) after the establishment of NUGS Moscow, the community bacame a merger between the students and workers living in Saint Petersburg. The purpose of the union was to foster great unity amongst all Ghanaians living in Saint Petersburg and to provide many opportunities ranging from part time jobs, summer jobs and resolution of document issues. Undoubtedly, the union since then has become the mouth piece of all Ghanains in Petersburg and offers great link between the Ghana Embassy, Moscow and the Ghanaian populace in Petersburg, Russia.


Daniel K. Oppong

Financial Status

The financial status of every union has always been the backbone of every union without which a union can barely stand. To run the Union, Nugs Petersburg/ Ghana Community members are tasked to make monthly contributions. These monthly monies are apportioned according to set down rules of the union where respective percentages of the total revenue collected go into :

1. Welfare
2. Football team
3. Visiting & media team
4. Annual Get-together
5. Graduation/Child naming and other ceremonies
6. Summer camps and Picnics.

The union also from time to time receives special sponsorships and Aids from persons and organizations.
